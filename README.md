# ResumeIQ — Resume Compatibility Analyzer

**ResumeIQ** is a modern, AI-powered recruitment tool designed to streamline the candidate screening process. By leveraging advanced Large Language Models (LLMs), it provides recruiters and hiring managers with deep insights into candidate suitability, technical skill alignment, and experience depth—all within a secure, browser-native environment.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🧠 Multi-Provider AI Engine
Connect to the world's most powerful AI models with ease. ResumeIQ supports a wide range of providers:
- **Google Gemini**: High-accuracy, long-context analysis.
- **Groq**: Lightning-fast inference for instant results.
- **NVIDIA NIM**: Enterprise-grade model performance.
- **OpenRouter**: Access to a vast library of open-source and proprietary models (Claude, Llama, Mistral).
- **Custom Endpoints**: Fully compatible with any OpenAI-standard API.

### 💬 Intelligent AI Assistant
Go beyond simple scoring. Our built-in chat assistant allows you to interact with your data:
- **Candidate Comparison**: Ask "Why is Candidate A a better fit for this role than Candidate B?" and get instant, tabulated comparisons.
- **Interview Prep**: Generate targeted interview questions based on a candidate's specific gaps or strengths.
- **Deep Dives**: Query specific details about a candidate's experience or educational background.

### 📊 Professional Analytics & Reporting
- **Automated Ranking**: Instantly sort candidates based on a weighted compatibility score.
- **Skill Mapping**: Visualize matched and missing skills with intuitive, color-coded badges.
- **Premium PDF Export**: Generate boardroom-ready hiring reports with consistent branding, detailed recruiter insights, and professional typography.

### 🛡️ Privacy & Security First
- **Browser-Native**: No server-side storage of your resumes. All processing happens between your browser and your chosen AI provider.
- **Session Security**: API keys are stored only in volatile session memory and are cleared as soon as the tab is closed.

## 📖 Quick Start Guide
1. **Open `index.html`** in any modern web browser.
2. **Setup Provider**: Click the "Click to set AI Provider" pill in the navigation bar. Select your preferred API and enter your key.
3. **Define the Role**: Paste the Job Description into the JD field. Be as detailed as possible for higher accuracy.
4. **Upload Resumes**: Drag and drop PDF, DOCX, or TXT resumes into the drop zone.
5. **Analyze**: Click "Analyze Candidates" and watch the AI rank them in real-time.
6. **Chat & Export**: Use the Chat Bubble to ask follow-up questions, then export the final report to PDF.

---

## 💡 Pro-Tips for Better Results
- **Context is King**: The more detailed your Job Description, the better the AI can distinguish between candidates.
- **Advanced Models**: For complex roles requiring nuanced judgment, we recommend using "Pro" or "Large" models (e.g., Gemini 1.5 Pro or Claude 3.5 Sonnet).
- **Markdown Tables**: The AI Assistant can generate tables to help you compare candidates side-by-side.
