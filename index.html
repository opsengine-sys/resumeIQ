1 Setup AI Provider

Select a provider and enter your API key. Keys are never stored on our servers; they stay in your browser session for maximum security. Use the links below to get keys instantly.

2 Define the Role

Paste a detailed JD. AI uses this as the "Source of Truth" to evaluate candidates. Include technical stacks, soft skills, and years of experience for higher accuracy.

3 Upload & Rank

Drop PDF, TXT, or DOCX files. The AI extracts text, maps it against the JD, and provides a compatibility score (0-100%) with specific matched/missing skill chips.

4 Chat & Export

Use the AI Assistant to compare candidates ("Why is Candidate A better than B?") or generate interview questions. Export a professional PDF report for your team.
